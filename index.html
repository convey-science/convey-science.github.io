About Us

Our Story

Susana Roque-Malo, PhD founded Convey Science LLC in 2021 to help researchers beautifully convey the stories behind their science.

Credentials

Roque-Malo holds a PhD in Civil Engineering (hydrology) from the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign. She has extensive experience providing graphics support for NSF, USDA, NIH, DOT, DOE, EDA, and other grant proposals, and leading peer-reviewed scientific journals.

Our Approach

At Convey Science, we blend scientific expertise with design excellence to create visuals that are both technically accurate and visually compelling. We work closely with our clients to understand their research goals and target audience, ensuring that every graphic effectively communicates the intended message.
